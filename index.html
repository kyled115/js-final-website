How do I become a "true Canadian"?

To become a true Canadian you need to start by always apologizing and always saying "Eh?" after a sentence. Most people think this is a cultural joke, but this is not even close to a joke.. We are dead serious when we state these things! We take these basic steps very seriously.

The Canadian Armed Forces

To keep our glorious country safe, our Armed Forces is here! The current form of the Canadian Armed Forces was founded on the 1st of February, 1968. The main HQ is based in Ottawa, The commander and chief of our armed forces is Governor General David Johnson. We have about 70 thousand active military personal ready to defend our country!
